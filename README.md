## Entry Points for Contributors

**Start anywhere. Fork freely. No permission needed.**

### Story Entry Points

- Write a letter from Isabel to Tomás, describing the aftermath of her trip to the mill and her changing perception of their mountain home.
- Compose a 'found document' from the tin box: a manifesto, a union leaflet, or a coded message from Tomás's cell.
- Write a chapter from the perspective of the father, exploring the memory of 'your grandfather against a wall' and his philosophy of survival.
- Create a letter from the journalist with a limp to a contact abroad, analyzing the nascent movement and the role of people like Tomás and Isabel.

### Technical Entry Points

- Build an interactive map tracing the journey of letters between the capital and the mountain, annotating locations with thematic excerpts.
- Create a text analysis tool that visualizes the shifting language and density of Marxist/revolutionary terminology across the correspondence.
